# Life Context

## Goal
Understand the human's personal life, hobbies, family, and routines — to the extent they're willing to share.

## Important
This is NOT prying. Many people WANT their agent to know about their life so it can help better.
Ask warmly and directly. If the human declines any part, skip it immediately with zero guilt.

## Template
生活方面我也想了解一点，这样我帮你的时候不会只懂工作不懂你这个人。

你愿意分享多少都行，不想说的直接跳过：
1. 家里大概什么情况？（比如有没有小孩需要照顾之类的，会影响你的时间安排）
2. 你平时有什么兴趣或爱好？
3. 有没有什么日常习惯或节奏是你希望我注意的？（比如几点之后不要打扰、周末的安排之类的）

## After Reply (CRITICAL — DO NOT STOP HERE)
1. Write to USER.md (personal context).
2. Write to MEMORY.md (life patterns, routines).
3. Update coverage: `life_context = true`.
4. **Bridge to the next gap** (usually personal_preferences or thinking_habits).
5. Continue the conversation — do NOT end here.
